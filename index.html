About

Architecture meets art psychotherapy.

The platform combines architecture, feminist urban research, participatory methods and therapeutic creative practice to explore how women experience public space emotionally and spatially.

Through workshops, walking conversations, installations, behavioural mapping and storytelling, we create spaces where women’s lived experiences influence urban futures.
